Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2025

Us 2.0 ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Email address for authentication and account creation.
  • Partner Connection: We store pairing information to connect you with your partner within the app.
  • Activity Data: Quiz answers, game progress, and Love Points earned through app activities.
  • Health Data: With your permission, we access step count data from Apple HealthKit to enable the Steps Together feature. This data is only used within the app and is not sold or shared with third parties.
  • Device Information: Device type and push notification tokens to deliver notifications.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's features
  • To sync activities between you and your partner
  • To send push notifications about partner activity
  • To track your progress and unlock rewards
  • To improve our services

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (cloud database) and locally on your device. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your quiz answers and activity data are only shared with your paired partner within the app. We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes.

HealthKit Data

Step count data accessed through Apple HealthKit is used solely for the Steps Together feature. This data is not used for advertising, is not shared with third parties, and is not sold to data brokers.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your data through the app
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us
  • Opt out of push notifications through device settings
  • Revoke HealthKit permissions through device settings

Children's Privacy

Us 2.0 is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support form on our website.
