6. **Natural Turn-Taking** - Every quiz has a "subject" whose answers are predicted by partner

## **üîÑ Core Quiz Flow (Knowledge Test Model)**

**All quiz formats follow this pattern:**

```
Step 1: Alice initiates quiz ‚Üí becomes the SUBJECT
Step 2: Alice answers questions ABOUT HERSELF
        Example: "What's MY favorite food?" ‚Üí "Pizza"
Step 3: Bob gets notification ‚Üí opens quiz as PREDICTOR
Step 4: Bob sees questions FROM ALICE'S PERSPECTIVE
        Example: "What's ALICE's favorite food?" ‚Üí Guesses "Pizza"
Step 5: System compares Alice's self-answers vs. Bob's predictions
Step 6: Match % = How well Bob knows Alice
Step 7: Next quiz ‚Üí Roles swap (Bob becomes subject, Alice predicts)
```

**Key Benefits:**
- Every question is used TWICE (once per person as subject)
- Natural turn-taking rhythm keeps it fair
- Tests depth of knowledge, not just similarity
- More romantic framing: "You really know me!"

## **üé≤ Quiz Type Expansion (8 Formats)**

### **1. Classic Match Quiz (Current)**
- **Flow:** Initiator answers 5 questions about themselves ‚Üí Partner predicts
- **Example:** Alice answers "My favorite food is Pizza" ‚Üí Bob guesses "Pizza"
- **Next Time:** Roles swap (Bob becomes subject, Alice predicts)
- **Unlock:** Available immediately
- **LP Reward:** 10-50 LP based on match %
- **Enhancement:** Add difficulty tiers (Level 1/2/3)

### **2. Speed Round**
- **Flow:** Subject answers 10 rapid questions about themselves ‚Üí Partner predicts with 10s time limit per question
- **Example:** Alice answers quickly ‚Üí Bob has 10 seconds to guess each
- **High-Energy:** Fast-paced knowledge test
- **Unlock:** After 5 Classic Quizzes
- **LP Reward:** 20-40 LP
- **Unique Mechanic:** Streak bonus (+5 LP per 3 consecutive correct predictions)

### **3. Deep Dive Session**
- **Flow:** Subject answers 15 questions in ONE category ‚Üí Partner predicts all 15
- **Example:** "Preferences" Deep Dive - Alice answers 15 preference questions ‚Üí Bob predicts
- **Deep Knowledge Test:** Tests expertise in specific area
- **Unlock:** After reaching Arena Tier 2 (Beach Villa)
- **LP Reward:** 60-120 LP
- **Unique Mechanic:** Category expertise tracking

### **4. Mystery Box**
- **Flow:** Subject doesn't know which categories will appear ‚Üí Answers 7 surprise questions ‚Üí Partner predicts
- **Example:** Alice gets mix of Favorites, Memories, Preferences (unknown to her) ‚Üí Bob predicts
- **Surprise Element:** Adds spontaneity
- **Unlock:** After 10 total quizzes
- **LP Reward:** 30-80 LP (variable based on difficulty)
- **Unique Mechanic:** Bonus LP for hard questions

### **5. Daily Pulse (Micro-Quiz)**
- **Flow:** ALTERNATING subject role daily
  - **Odd days (Mon/Wed/Fri/Sun):** Alice is subject ‚Üí Bob predicts
  - **Even days (Tue/Thu/Sat):** Bob is subject ‚Üí Alice predicts
- **Example Monday:** Alice answers "My ideal breakfast?" ‚Üí Bob predicts
- **Example Tuesday:** Bob answers "My weekend preference?" ‚Üí Alice predicts
- **Always available** (doesn't replace other formats)
- **LP Reward:** 5 LP participation + 5 LP match + 5 LP same-day = max 15 LP
- **Unique Mechanic:** Streak tracking and multipliers (7 days = +10 LP, 14 days = +25 LP, 30 days = +50 LP)
- **Design Goal:** 30-second daily habit with fair rotation

### **6. Would You Rather? (Hybrid Format)**
- **Flow:** BOTH answer scenario questions about themselves ‚Üí THEN predict partner's choice
- **Example:**
  - Question: "Would you rather: Beach vacation or Mountain retreat?"
  - Alice picks: "Beach" ‚Üí Then predicts Bob will pick "Mountain"
  - Bob picks: "Mountain" ‚Üí Then predicts Alice will pick "Beach"
  - Scoring: Points for correct prediction + bonus if choices align
- **Hybrid Mechanic:** Tests prediction AND discovers alignment
- **Unlock:** After 15 total quizzes
- **LP Reward:** 25-50 LP
- **Unique:** No "wrong" self-answers, only prediction accuracy matters

### **7. Timeline Challenge (Collaborative Format)**
- **Flow:** BOTH answer independently about shared memories ‚Üí Must match to score
- **Example:** "Where was our first date?"
  - Alice: "Coffee shop"
  - Bob: "Coffee shop"
  - Match = Points earned
- **Collaborative Recall:** Tests shared memory alignment
- **Unlock:** After 1 month paired
- **LP Reward:** 40-80 LP (only if BOTH answer correctly)
- **Unique Mechanic:** Perfect match on all questions = bonus LP

### **8. Reverse Quiz (Custom Content)**
- **Flow:** Each person writes 3 custom questions for partner ‚Üí Partner answers AS THEMSELVES ‚Üí Original writer had "correct answer" in mind
- **Example:**
  - Alice writes for Bob: "What would you do if you won the lottery?"
  - Bob answers: "Travel the world"
  - Alice's expected answer: "Travel the world" ‚Üí Match!
- **Most Intimate:** Fully personalized, saved to "Memory Vault"
- **Unlock:** After reaching Arena Tier 3 (Yacht Getaway)
- **LP Reward:** 80-150 LP
- **Unique:** User-generated content, permanent relationship archive

## **‚ö° Difficulty Tiers (3 Levels Per Question)**

### **Level 1: Warm-Up (Current Questions)**
- Surface-level, "getting to know you"
- Multiple choice with obvious answers
- Example: "What's my favorite color?"

### **Level 2: Challenge**
- Requires deeper knowledge/memory
- Less obvious options, closer answers
- Example: "What would I choose for a last meal?" (4 similar gourmet options)

### **Level 3: Expert**
- Nuanced, requires real intimacy
- "Why" questions, scenario-based
- Example: "If I had to pick between career opportunity and time with you, what would I prioritize RIGHT NOW?"

**Difficulty Selection:**
- Quiz initiator picks difficulty (or selects "Mixed")
- Higher difficulty = Higher LP rewards (1.5x for Challenge, 2x for Expert)
- Difficulty unlocks based on quiz count (Challenge after 5, Expert after 20)

### **"Other" Option (Always Available)**
- **All questions include 5 options** (4 specific choices + 1 "Other")
- **Context-aware wording:**
  - Standard questions: "Other / Something else"
  - Behavioral questions: "Other / It depends"
  - Seasonal/temporal: "Other / Varies"
  - Memory questions: "Other place"
- **Purpose:** Allows honest answers when preferences don't match provided options
- **Matching logic:** Both picking "Other" = match (shows understanding that partner doesn't fit predefined choices)
- **Conversation starter:** "Other" selections create natural discussion opportunities
- **Data quality:** Prevents forced inaccurate answers

## **üîÅ Daily/Weekly Engagement Mechanics**

### **Daily Pulse (Core Daily Habit):**
- **Single question** appears every 24 hours with **ALTERNATING subject role**
- **Rotation Logic:**
  - **Odd days (Mon/Wed/Fri/Sun):** Alice is subject (answers about herself) ‚Üí Bob predicts
  - **Even days (Tue/Thu/Sat):** Bob is subject (answers about himself) ‚Üí Alice predicts
- **Example Flow (Monday):**
  - Morning: Alice sees "What's YOUR ideal breakfast?" ‚Üí Answers "Pancakes"
  - Bob gets notification: "Alice answered today's Daily Pulse - guess her answer!"
  - Bob sees: "What's ALICE's ideal breakfast?" ‚Üí Guesses "Pancakes"
  - Both receive results: Match = 15 LP each, Mismatch = 5 LP each
- **Rewards:**
  - 5 LP for participation (subject answering + predictor guessing)
  - +5 LP if prediction matches
  - +5 LP if both complete on same day
  - Streak multiplier: 7 days = +10 LP, 14 days = +25 LP, 30 days = +50 LP
- **Design Goal:** Make checking the app daily rewarding but LOW friction (30 seconds)
- **Fairness:** Both partners are subject 3-4 days per week

### **Weekly Challenge:**
- Every Monday, new themed quiz assigned
- Example themes:
  - "Memory Lane Monday" (memories category)
  - "Future Friday" (dreams/goals)
  - "Wild Card Wednesday" (random mix)
- **Complete by Sunday = 50 LP bonus**
- Streak tracking (complete 4 consecutive weeks = badge)

### **Weekend Deep Dive:**
- Fridays unlock "Weekend Special" Deep Dive
- Extended session (10-15 questions)
- Higher LP reward (80-120 LP)
- Disappears Monday morning (creates urgency)

### **Monthly Quest:**
- Complete 4 quizzes in a specific category during the month
- Example: "March Challenge: Complete 4 'Future Dreams' quizzes"
- Reward: 200 LP + exclusive seasonal badge
- Announced on 1st of month with countdown

### **Notification Strategy:**
- Daily Pulse reminder (opt-in, customizable time)

## **Final Thoughts**

This expansion transforms quizzes from a **novelty** into a **core engagement loop**. The key is:

1. **Start simple** - Don't overwhelm new users
2. **Reward consistency** - Daily Pulse is the anchor with fair turn-taking
3. **Create curiosity** - Locked content = motivation
4. **Celebrate learning** - Every mismatch is an opportunity to know each other better
5. **Sustain with variety** - 8 formats + seasonal content = never boring
6. **Build rituals** - Daily/weekly patterns = habit formation
7. **Keep it fair** - Both partners are subject equally often (no one feels "tested" more than the other)

With 500+ questions, 8 formats, 18 categories, 40+ badges, and rotating seasonal content, you'll have **months of unique content** before users ever see repetition. Combined with the Daily Pulse habit (which can recycle questions after 6+ months), this system is designed for **long-term retention**.

### **The "Knowledge Test" Advantage:**

By keeping the **"How well do you know me?"** model instead of a similarity test, you get:
- **2x content value** - Every question is used twice (once per person as subject)
- **More romantic** - "You really know me!" feels better than "We're similar"
- **Natural conversation starters** - Mismatches reveal gaps in knowledge
- **Progressive deepening** - As relationships mature, predictions should get more accurate (measurable growth)
- **Fair rotation** - Daily Pulse alternates, longer quizzes swap initiators naturally

The magic is making couples feel like they're **learning each other deeply** through play, not being judged or compared. That's what will keep them coming back.
