Implementation Notes

These mockups demonstrate how daily quests can be integrated into the Activity Hub/Inbox to create a unified activity monitoring system. The key principle is that the inbox becomes the single source of truth for "what needs my attention" and "what have we accomplished together."

Key Design Elements

  • Quest Progress Indicator: Prominent banner at the top showing daily quest completion (2/3 complete, 67%)
  • Unified Activity Cards: Consistent design across all activity types with type badge, title, subtitle, and status
  • Status Badges: Color-coded badges (Orange = Your Turn, Gray = Waiting, Black = Completed)
  • Participant Avatars: Visual indication of who has completed each activity with checkmarks
  • LP Rewards: Shows potential rewards (üí∞ +30) for incomplete items, earned rewards after completion
  • Filter System: All, Your Turn, Completed, Pokes tabs for easy navigation
  • Chronological Order: Most recent activities first, grouped by date in completed view

Design Rationale

  • Home Screen vs Inbox: Home remains the dashboard with quick glance at quests; Inbox is for detailed monitoring
  • Daily Quest Integration: Quests appear alongside other activities, not in separate section
  • Visual Hierarchy: Type badges help distinguish quest types at a glance
  • Partner Awareness: Always shows who's completed what, fostering collaboration
  • Motivation: Visible LP rewards encourage completion
