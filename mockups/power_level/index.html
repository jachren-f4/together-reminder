Power Level Memory Challenge

HTML mockups designed to match TogetherRemind's minimalist black & white aesthetic with Playfair Display serif fonts and Inter sans-serif fonts.

About This Feature

Concept: A competitive memory challenge where couples test their recall of past quiz answers.

Gameplay: Each player answers 5 questions about their previous quiz responses. Correct answers charge their "power level." The player with the highest power level wins!

Rewards: Victory: 50 LP | Defeat: 20 LP

Eligibility: Requires 10+ completed quizzes to unlock (builds question pool from quiz history)
