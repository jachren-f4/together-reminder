Quest Badge Variants

Currently "YOUR TURN" is shown for both fresh quests and when partner has completed. These mockups explore different ways to distinguish these states and make the UI more informative.

Variant 1: START (Action-Oriented)
Fresh quest shows "START" - clear call to action. Partner completed shows "YOUR TURN" with urgency.
Fresh: START | Partner done: YOUR TURN | You done: Waiting for... | Both: COMPLETED
Variant 2: NEW (Status Badge)
Fresh quest shows "NEW" badge - highlights novelty. Partner completed shows "YOUR TURN".
Fresh: NEW | Partner done: YOUR TURN | You done: Waiting for... | Both: COMPLETED
Variant 3: Dual Initial Circles
Shows both partner initials with filled/empty states to indicate who has completed.
Fresh: [Y][T] empty | Partner done: [Y][T filled] | You done: [Y filled][T] | Both: [Y filled][T filled]
Variant 4: Progress Dots
Two dots showing completion progress - minimal and elegant.
Fresh: START ○○ | Partner done: YOUR TURN ○● | You done: WAITING ●○ | Both: DONE ●●
Variant 5: Contextual Text
More descriptive text that explains the current state clearly.
Fresh: "Begin together" | Partner done: "Taija is waiting" | You done: "Waiting for Taija" | Both: COMPLETED
