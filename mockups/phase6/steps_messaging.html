Phase 6: Steps Feature Rename

Root Cause

  • The word "together" fundamentally implies physical proximity
  • No amount of explanatory copy can overcome this semantic issue
  • Users think they need to walk side-by-side with their partner
  • Feature underutilized due to this core misunderstanding

Solution: Complete Rename + Compact Layout

  • Rename feature: "Steps Together" ‚Üí "Steps"
  • Remove "together" from ALL copy - not just "walk together"
  • Don't try to explain "together but not physically together" - just avoid the word
  • Compact intro screen to avoid scrolling:
    • ‚ùå Remove sneaker emoji (saves vertical space)
    • ‚ùå Remove Tips card (move to counter screen instead)
    • ‚úÖ Merge clarification into subtitle (single text block)
    • ‚úÖ Shorten "How it works" step text

Name & Copy Changes

Location Before After
Feature name "Steps Together" "Steps"
Intro headline "Walk together, earn together." "Walk more, earn more."
Intro subtitle (varies) "Walk more, earn more. Your steps automatically combine with your partner's. You don't need to be next to each other!"
Counter message "Keep walking together!" "Keep walking! Every step counts toward your daily goal."
Tips "Take a walk together after dinner" "Take a walk after dinner" / "Go for a morning walk"
Clarification (none - separate banner) (merged into subtitle - see above)

Key Messaging Principles

  • Never use "together": It implies physical proximity
  • Emphasize "automatic": Steps combine/sync without coordination
  • Focus on individual action: "Your steps count"
  • Partner mentioned only in context of combining: "combine with your partner's"

Files to Modify

  • app/lib/screens/steps_intro_screen.dart - Feature name, tagline, how it works, tips
  • app/lib/screens/steps_counter_screen.dart - Encouragement message, tips
  • app/lib/widgets/brand/us2/us2_bottom_nav.dart - Nav label
  • Any other file referencing "Steps Together" in UI copy

Words to Avoid

  • Never use: "together", "as a team", "sync up", "at the same time", "side-by-side"
  • Safe to use: "combine", "add up", "automatically", "sync automatically", "your steps"
