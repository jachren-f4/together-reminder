Implementation Notes

  • status-fresh - Used for quests that haven't been started by either partner
  • status-first-player - Used for turn-based games (Linked, Word Search) showing who goes first based on couple preferences
  • status-your-turn - Partner has completed their part, waiting for you. Shows pulsing indicator for urgency.
  • status-urgent - For turn-based games when partner is actively waiting. Uses gradient background for emphasis.
  • status-waiting - You've completed, waiting for partner. Shows animated dots.
  • status-results - Both completed but results not yet viewed. Glowing animation draws attention.
  • status-completed - Quest fully complete with green checkmark.
  • status-locked - Quest is locked until unlock criteria is met. Card is grayscale.
  • Turn-based games (Linked, Word Search) use currentTurnUserId from active match to determine whose turn it is.
  • Standard quests use userCompletions array to track who has completed.
