Status Badge Reference (us2_quest_card.dart)

1
Fresh: Neither player started. Badge: white 25% bg, sparkle icon, "Begin together".
2
First Player: Turn-based game, no active match. Badge: white 25% bg, gradient initial circle, "[Name] goes first".
3
Your Turn: Partner completed their part. Badge: white bg, shadow, salmon text, salmon initial, pulse dot.
4
Urgent: Partner is actively waiting (turn-based). Badge: gradient bg, glow shadow, white text, white initial.
5
Waiting: User completed, waiting for partner. Badge: white 25% bg, white initial (salmon text), animated dots.
6
Results Ready: Both completed, pending results. Badge: white bg, glow animation, sparkle icons, salmon text.
7
Completed: Both finished, results viewed. Badge: white bg, green check circle (18x18), green text.
8
Locked: Feature not unlocked. Image: grey gradient + lock overlay (48px white circle). Badge: black 20% bg, lock icon, criteria text.
9
On Cooldown: Puzzle completed, waiting for reset. Badge: white 90% bg, grey border, schedule icon, time remaining.
