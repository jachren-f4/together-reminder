Surfacing Therapeutic Metadata

Exploring ways to bring quiz insights into the app experience

Available Metadata from Quizzes

rationale

Why this question matters psychologically. The deeper meaning behind each question.

framework

Psychological basis: Gottman, Attachment Theory, Love Languages, etc.

whenDifferent

Guidance when partners answer differently. Conversation starters.

whenSame

Insight when partners match. Celebrates alignment.

journalPrompt

Reflection question for deeper exploration. Personal growth tool.

dimension + poleMapping

Personality profiling data. Tracks patterns across quizzes.

Additional Ideas

Framework Learning Cards

Unlock mini-lessons about Gottman, Attachment Theory, etc. as users complete more quizzes. "You've explored 5 questions about attachment. Here's what attachment theory says about your pattern..."

Couple's Therapy Topics

For couples in therapy: export a summary of key differences with whenDifferent insights to discuss with their therapist. "Here are 3 areas where you see things differently..."

Growth Tracking Over Time

If users re-take quizzes, show how their answers (and alignment) has changed. "6 months ago you said X, now you say Y. Here's what that shift might mean..."

Date Night Conversation Deck

Turn journal prompts into a "conversation card" feature for date nights. Swipe through prompts, discuss together, mark favorites.

Personalized Tips Based on Patterns

After enough data: "Based on your quizzes, you tend toward anxious attachment while your partner leans avoidant. Here are 3 things that might help..."
