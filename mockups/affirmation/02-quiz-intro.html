Do You Keep Secrets?

Goal
Gain awareness of strength and growth areas in how you connect emotionally as a couple.
Research
Having some secrets is completely normal in relationships. However, the importance lies in understanding your values and expectations around secrecy.

A 2023 study in the journal Personal Relationships found that the quality of secrecy—not just its presence—affects relationship satisfaction. Partners who clearly communicate their boundaries around privacy tend to report higher trust and intimacy.
