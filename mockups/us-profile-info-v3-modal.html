Love Languages
How you give & receive love

Based on Dr. Gary Chapman's framework, your love language is how you naturally express and feel love.

  • Words of Affirmation - Verbal compliments and encouragement
  • Quality Time - Undivided attention and presence
  • Receiving Gifts - Thoughtful presents and symbols of love
  • Acts of Service - Helpful actions that ease your load
  • Physical Touch - Hugs, holding hands, physical closeness
