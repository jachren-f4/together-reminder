Design Decisions

  • Connection Bar: Magnet images replace tier emojis (no labels). Both magnets fully visible (no graying).
  • Connection Bar (New User): No left magnet - heart starts at far left, first destination on right.
  • Profile Section: Always shows 6 badges - collected ones first, then grayed out to fill.
  • Celebration: Confetti SHOWER falls from top (in front of magnet), less rounded magnet (8px), single stat.
  • "Add to Collection": Navigates to Collection View with new magnet highlighted.
  • Polaroid Style: White border like instant photos - nostalgic travel memory feel.
