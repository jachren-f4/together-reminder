Phase 1.4: LP Intro Overlay Improvements

Issues Addressed

  • Emoji overload: 3 different emoji types with competing animations
  • Animation chaos: 5+ simultaneous animations (pulse, bounce, pop, fade)
  • Typography mess: 4 font sizes, italic mixed with regular
  • Unclear messaging: "Fill your journey bar" but what does LP mean?

Before vs After Comparison

Element Before After
Header icon üíó emoji (48px, pulsing) Custom LP coin icon (72px, static)

Key Design Decisions

  • LP coin icon: Creates brand recognition - "LP" text inside golden coin
  • Progress bar as hero: The fill animation IS the celebration (no extra bouncing hearts)
  • Clear hierarchy: Title ‚Üí Subtitle ‚Üí Earned amount ‚Üí Progress ‚Üí CTA
  • Reward preview: Shows destination with "X LP to go" (concrete goal)

Animation Spec

  • Overlay fade: 400ms ease-out
  • Card scale: 0.9 ‚Üí 1.0, 300ms ease-out
  • Progress fill: 0% ‚Üí 15%, 1500ms ease-out (starts after 500ms delay)
  • No other animations - let the progress bar be the focus
