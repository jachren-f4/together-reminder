Phase 2.2: LP Reward Clarity

Issue Identified

  • Users confused why they get LP even when answers don't match
  • Expectation: "LP = alignment score"
  • Reality: "LP = participation reward"
  • No explanation of what triggers LP

Solution: Participation-Based LP

  • LP Formula: Fixed 30 LP per quiz completion
  • Psychology: Low pressure - playing together is what matters
  • Message: Differences in answers are OK in relationships

Changes

  • Dedicated LP section: Separate from match score with clear explanation
  • Explanation text: "You earned LP from participation!"
  • Visual separation: LP in its own card, score above
  • Remove floating badge: No more bouncing "+30 LP" that confuses the message
  • First classic quiz only: LP explanation card only shown on first classic quiz results, then never again

LP Card Behavior

  • Appears on first classic quiz results only - not on Welcome Quiz, not on subsequent quizzes
  • Stored in Hive: hasSeenLpExplanationCard: true
  • After first quiz, just show the "+30 LP" badge inline (no explanation card)
  • Can reuse same flag check as instruction banner, or use separate flag
