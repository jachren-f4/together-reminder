Implementation Notes

  • Trigger: Show after user completes their FIRST classic quiz (before waiting screen)
  • Only once: Track in Hive with a flag like hasSeenNotificationPrompt
  • iOS: Tapping "Enable" triggers the native iOS permission dialog
  • Android 13+: Similar native permission dialog; Android 12 and below auto-grants
  • "Maybe Later": Dismisses popup, user can enable later in Settings
  • Context: Showing this right after quiz completion makes the value proposition clear - they want to know when their partner finishes!
  • Alternative timing: Could also show on the waiting screen itself, with copy like "Want to know when they finish?"
