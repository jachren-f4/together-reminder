Design Notes

  • Repair section only appears on dimensions marked as "Different" or "Complementary"
  • Collapsed by default - subtle dashed border invites curiosity
  • Uses warm colors to feel supportive, not clinical
  • Scripts are personalized with actual partner names
  • Recognition prompt helps couples identify the pattern
  • Pro tip gives actionable, concrete suggestion
  • Language is non-blaming: "when this causes friction" not "when you fight"
