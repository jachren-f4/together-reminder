Design Notes

  • High-stakes card has distinct visual treatment (border, badge)
  • Replaces simple "Try This" with multi-step approach
  • Acknowledgment validates the difficulty ("There's no quick answer")
  • Counselor prompt is optional, non-alarming, framed as optimization
  • Medium stakes get timing guidance but not full scaffolding
  • Light stakes stay simple - quick action is appropriate
  • Badge uses "Big Topic" not "High Stakes" (less clinical)
