When to Show Growth Edges
‚úÖ Show when:
  • Partner sees a positive trait you don't claim
  • Self-description differs from partner's view (neutral tone)
  • Comparison reveals hidden strength
‚ùå Never show when:
  • Gap could feel critical ("You said confident, they said insecure")
  • Difference implies blame or shortcoming
  • Topic is sensitive (intimacy, conflict patterns)

Design Notes

  • Uses blue tones to feel curious/exploratory, not alarming
  • Always frames as "interesting" not "incorrect"
  • Provides specific question to ask ‚Äî lowers barrier to conversation
  • Only shows gaps that reveal hidden positives or neutral differences
  • Data source: Compare "You or Me" answers + self-description questions
  • Reassurance note prevents defensive reaction
  • Never implies one person is "right" about the other
